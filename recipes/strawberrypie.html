Strawberry pie

Description

This pie is my favorite and it's very easy to make. I got the recipe many years ago in North Jersey from the Sussex County Strawberry Farm.

Ingredients

  • 1 quart fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • ¾ cup water
  • ½ cup heavy whipping cream

Steps

  1. Arrange 1/2 of strawberries in baked pastry shell then place the remaining strawberries in a medium saucepan.
  2. Add sugar to the strawberries in the saucepan, place over medium heat and bring to a boil whilst stirring frequently.
  3. Whisk cornstarch and water together in a small bowl. Gradually stir cornstarch mixture into boiling strawberry mixture. Reduce heat and simmer mixture until thickened for about 10 minutes whilst stirring constantly.
  4. Pour cooked strawberry mixture over berries in pastry shell. Refrigerate until chilled and set for at least 3 hours.
  5. Before serving, whip cream in a medium bowl until soft peaks form. Serve each slice of pie with a dollop of whipped cream.
